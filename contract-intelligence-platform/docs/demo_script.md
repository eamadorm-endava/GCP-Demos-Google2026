
# Nexus: Supply Chain Contract Intelligence - Live Demo Script

**Duration:** ~10 Minutes
**Roles:**
1.  **Legal Counsel / Procurement Manager:** Portfolio oversight & contract review
2.  **Dev/Admin:** Engineering Lead (Architecture & Configuration)
---

**About The Demo**
This demo illustrates how **Gemini Enterprise** transforms unstructured supplier agreements into actionable intelligence. Instead of manually reviewing hundreds of pages of legal PDFs, we showcase an **AI-Powered Contract Intelligence Platform** that extracts key clauses, assesses risk, and provides a natural language query interface to surface insights from an entire contract portfolio.

---

## Intro: Demo Vision & Context (The Hook)

**The Problem**
"Welcome to Nexus: Supply Chain Contract Intelligence. In manufacturing and logistics, supplier contracts are the lifeblood of operations—yet most organizations store them as static PDFs on a shared drive. When a supply chain disruption hits, nobody can quickly answer: 'Which contracts have uncapped liability?' or 'Which agreements expire in the next 90 days?' Today, we change that."

**The Tech (Vision & Logic)**
"Powered by Gemini Enterprise, Nexus uses **Document AI** to parse and extract every clause, party, and obligation from complex legal agreements. **Gemini Pro** then synthesizes this across the entire portfolio to generate risk scores, executive summaries, and answer natural language questions in real time."

**The Value (Agility)**
"With **Generative Contract Intelligence**, what used to take a legal team days of manual review—reading clauses, flagging risks, tracking renewals—now happens in seconds. We turn a filing cabinet of PDFs into a searchable, scored, actionable intelligence layer."

**The Transition**
"From static document storage to intelligent contract governance—let's see the platform in action."

## Part 1: The Portfolio Dashboard (The Big Picture)

**Landing Page**

* **Action:** Start on the **SupplyLens** Portfolio Dashboard (default landing page).
* **Narrator:** "I land on SupplyLens—our portfolio command center. At a glance, I see the health of our entire supplier contract landscape."

**Supply Chain Intelligence Banner**

* **Action:** Point to the **Supply Chain Intelligence** banner at the top, showing critical AI recommendations (e.g., "Diversify Critical T1 Supply", "De-risk Joint Development Agreement", "Standardize Lower Tier Contracts").
* **Narrator:** "Before I even start browsing, the AI has already surfaced its top recommendations. These are priority actions generated by analyzing the entire portfolio—diversify critical suppliers, de-risk joint development agreements, and standardize lower-tier contracts."

**KPI Widgets**

* **Action:** Point to the four KPI cards: **Active Suppliers (9)**, **Avg Supplier Risk (4.8)**, **Renewals 90D (0)**, and **Annual Spend Volume ($245M)**.
* **Narrator:** "Four critical numbers: 9 active supplier contracts, an average risk index of 4.8 out of 10, zero renewals coming due in 90 days, and $245 million in committed annual spend. This is my executive summary in four seconds."

**Risk & Analytics Charts**

* **Action:** Point to the interactive charts: **Risk Index by Category**, **Portfolio Risk Spread**, and **Legal Jurisdiction** distribution.
* **Narrator:** "Below, I see risk broken down by category—Operational, Financial, Regulatory. The Portfolio Spread chart shows how our contracts cluster across industries. And the Jurisdiction chart tells me which governing laws apply—critical for compliance planning."

**Industry & Type Filters**

* **Action:** Use the **Sector filter** dropdown to select a specific industry (e.g., "Healthcare" or "FSI"). Point to the **Type filter** as well.
* **Narrator:** "I can pivot the entire dashboard by industry sector or contract type. If I'm preparing for a healthcare compliance audit, I filter to Healthcare and instantly see only relevant contracts."

## Part 2: AI-Powered Search & Natural Language Query

**[TRANSITION TALK TRACK]**
*"The dashboard gives me the big picture. But what if I have a specific question? I shouldn't have to open 30 PDFs to find the answer. That's where our AI Search comes in."*

**NLQ Search Bar**

* **Action:** Click into the **search bar** at the top of the dashboard. Type: *"Which contracts have uncapped liability?"*
* **Narrator:** "Instead of keyword matching, I'm asking a semantic question. The AI interprets my intent—it understands 'uncapped liability' means no dollar cap on the indemnification clause."
* **Action:** Press Enter. Watch the contract library filter down to matching results.
* **Narrator:** "In seconds, the portfolio is filtered to only the contracts where the AI detected uncapped liability terms. No manual reading. No missed clauses."

**AI Interpretation Panel**

* **Action:** Point to the AI response panel showing the interpretation and matched contract IDs.
* **Narrator:** "The AI shows me exactly how it interpreted my query and which contracts matched. Full transparency—I can verify every result."

## Part 3: Contract Deep Dive (The Intelligence Layer)

**[TRANSITION TALK TRACK]**
*"We've found the risky contracts. Now let's drill into one and see how the AI dissects a complex legal agreement."*

**Contract Detail View**

* **Action:** Click on a contract card (e.g., **Strategic Semiconductor Supply MSA**). Land on the Contract Detail page.
* **Narrator:** "I click into the Strategic Semiconductor Supply MSA. The AI has already done the heavy lifting."

**AI Summary & Risk Score**

* **Action:** Point to the **AI-generated Executive Summary** and the **Risk Score** badge (e.g., 8/10).
* **Narrator:** "At the top, a Gemini-generated executive summary tells me what this contract is about—in plain language, not legalese. The Risk Score is 8 out of 10—High Risk. The AI has read every clause and assessed the exposure."

**Document Intelligence Viewer**

* **Action:** Click the **"Open Full Document"** or **"View Full Intelligence"** button to see the Document Intelligence Viewer.
* **Narrator:** "The Document Intelligence Viewer is our most advanced feature. On one side, I see the digital twin of the original document. On the other, the AI has extracted and categorized every significant element."

**Key Clauses Analysis**

* **Action:** Point to the extracted **Key Clauses** section—Liability, Force Majeure, Termination, Intellectual Property.
* **Narrator:** "Each key clause is extracted,  highlighted, and risk-rated. The Liability clause is flagged as High Risk because the indemnification is uncapped. The Force Majeure clause has medium risk—it covers natural disasters but not pandemics. This is clause-by-clause intelligence that would take a paralegal hours."

**AI Extracted Data**

* **Action:** Point to the **AI Extracted Data** panel showing parties, dates, governing law.
* **Narrator:** "The AI has also extracted structured metadata—parties, effective dates, governing law, renewal terms. All automatically parsed from unstructured PDF text."

## Part 4: Workflow Tools (Operationalizing Intelligence)

**[TRANSITION TALK TRACK]**
*"Intelligence without action is just data. Let's see how Nexus turns contract insights into operational workflows."*

**Due Diligence Checklist**

* **Action:** Navigate to the **Due Diligence Checklist** tab within the contract detail.
* **Narrator:** "For each contract type, the platform generates a dynamic Due Diligence Checklist. For this MSA, it checks: Has the financial exposure been reviewed? Are the force majeure terms acceptable? Is the governing law appropriate for our jurisdiction? I can check off items as my team completes each review step."
* **Action:** Click a checkbox to mark an item as complete.

**Tagging System**

* **Action:** Point to the **Tags** on the contract card or detail view. Add a new tag (e.g., "Needs Legal Review").
* **Narrator:** "I can apply custom tags for workflow tracking. I tag this as 'Needs Legal Review'—now anyone on the team can filter by that tag to pick up the work."

**Renewal Reminder Settings**

* **Action:** Navigate to the **Renewal Reminders** section.
* **Narrator:** "Finally, the platform tracks renewal dates and can alert us before critical contracts lapse. No more missed renewals costing us leverage in renegotiations."
