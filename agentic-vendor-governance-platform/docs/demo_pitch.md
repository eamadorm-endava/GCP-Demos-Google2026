**Target Persona:** Vignesh S. (Procurement Ops / C-Suite)
**Script:** 
"Vignesh, as you transition to a multi-vendor ecosystem—pitting incumbents like Globant against hungry challengers like TechFlow—you face a $450,000 problem. Currently, you’re paying vendors to 'grade their own homework' via manual self-reporting. You're flying blind, relying on their PowerPoints. 

Today, we change that. We’re moving from 'Trust but Verify' to 'Automated Governance.' We’re replacing manual overhead with an Agentic Layer that never sleeps, never misses a line item, and always stays neutral."

## 2. The Command Center (The "Visual")
**Action:** Open Dashboard.
**Script:** 
"This is your Command Center. For the first time, you see Vendor A vs. Vendor B in real-time. Look at the 'Head-to-Head' widget. While Globant has higher velocity, our AI detects their quality is dipping compared to TechFlow. You don't need to wait for a monthly meeting to know this—the Agentic Feed on the right alerted us two hours ago that Globant’s uptime just hit a critical threshold."

## 3. Financial Hub (The "ROI")
**Action:** Navigate to Financial Audit and show a Flagged Invoice.
**Script:** 
"Let’s talk money. In the old world, a Senior Architect billing 60 hours in a week would hide in a 50-page PDF. Here, the Auditor Agent caught it instantly. By cross-referencing the MSA Rate Card in real-time, the platform just flagged a $5,000 discrepancy. The tool doesn't just manage vendors; it pays for itself by catching overbilling before the check is even signed."

## 4. Meeting Hub (The "Efficiency")

"Finally, the 'QBR Prep' nightmare. Your team used to spend two weeks manually pulling Jira tickets and formatting slides. Watch this. In 10 seconds, we have a complete strategic analysis ready for Google Slides. You’ve just reclaimed 80 hours of your team's time."
