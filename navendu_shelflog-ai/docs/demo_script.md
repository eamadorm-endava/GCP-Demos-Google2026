
# ShelfLogic AI - Live Demo Script

**Duration:** ~10 Minutes
**Roles:**
1.  **Sarah:** Regional Operations Manager (Strategy & Network Health)
2.  **Alex:** Store Manager (Execution)
3.  **Dev/Admin:** Engineering Lead (Architecture & Configuration)

**Demo Vision & Gemini Enterprise Context:**
This demo illustrates how **Gemini Enterprise** empowers retailers to build next-generation applications that transcend traditional dashboards. Instead of static reporting, we showcase an **Autonomous Merchandising Agent** that moves from *Descriptive Analytics* (what happened) to *Prescriptive AI* (what to do).

Key capabilities demonstrated:
*   **Multimodal Reasoning:** Using Gemini Vision to process real-time shelf-edge camera feeds for planogram compliance.
*   **Complex Reasoning:** Leveraging Gemini Pro to synthesize disparate data points (demographics, weather, sales velocity) into coherent strategic narratives.
*   **Generative Agility:** Using Generative AI to instantly create and configure new industry vertical templates, showing the platform's extensibility.

---

## Part 1: The Strategic View (HQ Role)

**Goal:** Demonstrate high-level network visibility, autonomous opportunity detection, and the "Agent" capabilities.

**0:00 - Login Screen**
*   **Action:** Start on the Login Page.
*   **Narrator:** "Welcome to ShelfLogic AI. Traditionally, managing a retail network means wading through spreadsheets of stale data. Today, we're going to see how an Autonomous Agent can not just report problems, but prescribe solutions."
*   **Action:** Click **[HQ Administrator]**.

**0:30 - HQ Dashboard (The Command Center)**
*   **Action:** Land on Dashboard. Point out the **Impact Simulator**.
*   **Narrator:** "I log in as Sarah, the Regional VP. The first thing I see is the 'Morning Briefing'. The AI has analyzed my entire network overnight."
*   **Action:** Drag the **Impact Simulator** slider from 0% to 50%.
*   **Narrator:** "I can simulate adoption. If we execute 50% of the agent's recommendations, we project a $1.2M revenue lift this quarter."
*   **Action:** Scroll down to the **Optimization Pipeline**.
*   **Narrator:** "Below, I have a prioritized feed of 'Opportunities'. These aren't just alerts; they are fully packaged action plans."

**1:30 - Analysis: Assortment Swap (Digital Twins & Copilot)**
*   **Action:** Click **[Analyze]** on an *Assortment Swap* card (e.g., for Dubuque).
*   **Narrator:** "Let's look at this Assortment Swap. The agent has flagged a low-performing Schnapps for delisting."
*   **Action:** Hover over the **Map** and **Digital Twin** rationale.
*   **Narrator:** "It hasn't just guessed a replacement. It found a 'Digital Twin' store—Davenport—which matches Dubuque's demographics by 94%, but sells huge volumes of this Apple Liqueur."
*   **Action:** Type into the **AI Copilot** chat on the right: *"Why is the match score so high?"* or *"What is the risk?"*
*   **Narrator:** "If I'm skeptical, I can ask the AI Advisor directly. It explains the specific demographic vectors driving this recommendation."
*   **Action:** Click **[Execute Planogram Update]**.
*   **Narrator:** "The logic holds. I approve the swap instantly."

**3:00 - Analysis: Price Optimization (The Revenue Hill)**
*   **Action:** Return to Dashboard, click **[Analyze]** on a *Price Optimization* card.
*   **Narrator:** "Pricing is another lever. Here, the model detected that this Vodka is price *inelastic*."
*   **Action:** Show the **Revenue Curve** chart.
*   **Narrator:** "We can raise the price by 8% to capture margin without hurting volume. The chart shows the optimal point on the 'Revenue Hill'."

## Part 2: Network & Vision Intelligence

**Goal:** Show deep-dive capabilities into specific stores and real-time IoT integration.

**4:00 - Store Network & Details**
*   **Action:** Click **[Stores]** in the sidebar.
*   **Narrator:** "Now, let's look at the network health."
*   **Action:** Click on **Dubuque** card.
*   **Narrator:** "Drilling into Dubuque, I get a holistic view."
*   **Action:** Click **[Sales & Margin]** tab to show the **BCG Matrix**.
*   **Narrator:** "I can see their Product Portfolio Matrix—identifying 'Cash Cows' versus 'Dogs'. And below, the Hourly Heatmap helps with labor scheduling."

**5:00 - Shelf Vision (Real-Time IoT)**
*   **Action:** Click **[Vision]** in the sidebar.
*   **Narrator:** "This is our most advanced feature: Shelf Vision. We ingest real-time feeds from shelf-edge cameras."
*   **Action:** Select a node with a **VIOLATION** (e.g., Aisle 4 or Aisle 8).
*   **Narrator:** "The system compares the live pixel stream against the 'Master Planogram'. Here, it detected a violation—the Apple Liqueur we just approved is missing."
*   **Action:** Click **[Verify Fix]** (simulates scanning).
*   **Narrator:** "The manager can fix it, trigger a re-scan, and the AI verifies compliance in real-time."

## Part 3: The "Glass Box" (Trust & Explainability)

**6:00 - ML Strategy Lab**
*   **Action:** Click **[ML Lab]** in the sidebar.
*   **Narrator:** "We don't believe in black boxes. The ML Strategy Lab allows our data scientists to audit the algorithms."
*   **Action:** Toggle between **Clustering** (PCA visualization) and **Pricing** (Demand Curves). Adjust the sliders (Sensitivity/Volatility).
*   **Narrator:** "We can stress-test the model. What happens if customer price sensitivity doubles? What if market volatility spikes? The agent adapts."
