
# Sentinel AI: Autonomous Risk Assurance - Live Demo Script

**Duration:** ~10 Minutes
**Roles:**
1.  **Audit Lead / CISO:** Risk & Compliance Executive (Strategic Oversight)
2.  **Dev/Admin:** Engineering Lead (Architecture & Configuration)
---

**About The Demo**
This demo illustrates how **Gemini Enterprise** empowers risk and compliance teams to deploy a fleet of **Autonomous AI Agents** that systematically verify organizational controls. Instead of sampling 1% of data once a year, we showcase **Continuous Assessment** where specialized digital auditors work 24/7 to assess 100% of the environment.

---

## Intro: Demo Vision & Context (The Hook)

**The Problem**
"Welcome to Sentinel AI. The fundamental problem in Risk and Compliance today is **capacity**. We have thousands of controls, millions of transactions, and diverse systems like Cloud, HR, and Finance. But we only have a handful of human auditors. This leads to the 'Audit Gap': we rely on sampling 1% of the data once a year, leaving 99% of the risk unassessed."

**The Tech (Vision & Logic)**
"Powered by Gemini Enterprise, Sentinel AI deploys **Specialized Agent Fleets**—an IAM Agent for identity risks, a Forensic Agent for financial anomalies, a Compliance Agent for evidence collection—each with deep domain expertise. **Gemini Pro** performs autonomous cross-referencing across systems, reasoning about complex policy violations that would take a human auditor days."

**The Value (Agility)**
"With **Autonomous Assurance**, we're moving from annual spot-checks to continuous, real-time verification. The agents don't sample—they assess everything, generate cryptographically signed audit trails, and produce regulator-ready reports."

**The Transition**
"From retroactive auditing to autonomous assurance—let's see the agents in action."

## Part 1: The Welcome & Launch

**Welcome Modal**

* **Action:** Start on the **Welcome Modal** displaying "Agentic Governance."
* **Narrator:** "When Sentinel AI loads, the Welcome Modal introduces the platform's core capabilities: Continuous Compliance Monitoring, Zero-Touch Autonomous Execution, and Immutable Audit Trails."

**Active Agent Fleet**

* **Action:** Point to the **Active Agent Fleet** section in the modal, listing agent types: **Identity (Access Control)**, **Compliance (Audit Evidence)**, and **Forensics (Anomaly Detection)**.
* **Narrator:** "We can see the agent fleet ready for deployment. Each agent specializes in a different risk domain—Identity, Compliance, and Forensics. These aren't generic bots; they are purpose-built digital auditors."

**Launch Simulation**

* **Action:** Click the **[Launch Simulation]** button.
* **Narrator:** "Let's launch the simulation and see the Command Center."

---

## Part 2: The Command Center (Managing the Fleet)

**Risk Dashboard**

* **Action:** Land on the **Command Center** view.
* **Narrator:** "Welcome to the Command Center. This is where we manage our agent workforce. Unlike a static dashboard, this shows live assessment activity."

**Top-Level Metrics**

* **Action:** Point to the top metrics: **Control Coverage**, **Effectiveness Rate**, and **Active Agents**.
* **Narrator:** "At a glance: control coverage across the organization, the overall effectiveness rate of assessed controls, and the number of active agents currently deployed. This is continuous assessment in real time."

**Risk Feed**

* **Action:** Point to the Risk Feed listing critical issues (e.g., "Excessive Privileged Access," "Toxic Access Combinations").
* **Narrator:** "The feed shows identified risks, each with severity levels and affected systems. The agent has already flagged 'Excessive Privileged Access' as critical—someone has admin rights across multiple systems without a valid approval ticket."

**Deploy Agent**

* **Action:** Click the **[Deploy Agent]** button on a risk card (e.g., "Toxic Access Combinations").
* **Narrator:** "Let's deploy an IAM Agent to assess 'Toxic Access'—the risk that someone can both create and pay a fake vendor. I click Deploy."

---

## Part 3: Autonomous Reasoning (The Live Assessment)

**[TRANSITION TALK TRACK]**
*"This is the moment that separates Sentinel from every other GRC tool. We're not running a script—we're watching an autonomous agent think."*

**Activity Visualizer & Terminal**

* **Action:** Point to the **Activity Visualizer** on the left side of the screen.
* **Narrator:** "On the left, the Agent is autonomously navigating our infrastructure. It's connecting to HR to see who is active, and Security to see who has admin rights. Watch the connections light up in real time."

* **Action:** Point to the **Terminal** on the right side showing the agent's reasoning log.
* **Narrator:** "On the right, the Agent's Reasoning Terminal. It's not just matching keywords. It's asking: 'Does J.Doe have conflicting roles?' It found a conflict. Now it's checking if there was a valid ticket approving that conflict. This is the kind of complex cross-referencing that usually takes a human days."

**Assessment Completion**

* **Action:** Wait for the assessment to complete. The status shows "Deficiency" or "Effective."
* **Narrator:** "The Agent has finished the assessment in seconds—a comprehensive audit of the entire user population."

**View Report**

* **Action:** Click **[View Report]** when it appears.
* **Narrator:** "It generated an Internal Audit Memo. Scroll to the bottom—the **Audit Trail**. The Agent has cryptographically signed its work. This serves as immutable proof of compliance. You can hand this directly to your regulators."

**Agent Portfolio View**

* **Action:** Click **[Agent Portfolio]** in the sidebar (under System Config section).
